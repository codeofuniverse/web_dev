Ration Card Categories

  • APL (Above Poverty Line): Standard pricing.
    Note: Income exceeds the state-defined threshold.
  • BPL (Below Poverty Line): Subsidized rates for essential goods.
    Note: Priority status for monthly wheat and rice allocation.
  • Antyodaya (AAY): Highly subsidized rates for the poorest families.
    Note: Eligible for 35kg of food grains per month.
